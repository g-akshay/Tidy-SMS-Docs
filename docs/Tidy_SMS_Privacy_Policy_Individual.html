3. WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT

3.1 Information You Provide

We use SEND_SMS and READ_SMS permissions to provide the core functionality of organizing and managing your messages.

When you use Tidy SMS, you grant permission to access:

  • SMS Messages: To read and organize your text messages into categories (Personal, Transactional, OTP, Spam, Promotions)
  • Contacts: To identify message senders and display contact names in your conversations

Important: I do not store your SMS messages or contacts on any servers. All processing happens locally on your device only.

3.2 Information Automatically Collected

I may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Android OS version, device model, app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (filters applied, categories used)
  • Crash and Error Data: To help fix bugs and improve app stability
  • Analytics Data: Through Firebase Analytics (see Section 6 below)

4. HOW YOUR DATA IS PROCESSED AND STORED

4.1 Local-First Architecture

Your SMS messages and contacts never leave your device. They are not uploaded to servers, cloud storage, or shared with anyone. All message categorization and filtering happens locally on your phone using machine learning models that run directly on your device.

4.2 What I Keep on My Servers

I only store:

  • Bug Reports: If you voluntarily send crash reports or feature requests
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized usage patterns (e.g., "app opened 5 times") through Firebase
  • Crash Reports: Error logs to help improve app stability

This data is never linked to your name, phone number, or any identifying information.

4.3 Data Retention

  • Local SMS/Contacts: Stored on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • Server-Side Data: Automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Crash Reports: Deleted after 30 days
  • Analytics Data: Kept for 14 months (aggregated only)

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

6.1 Firebase Analytics (Google)

I use Firebase by Google to understand how the app is used. Firebase collects anonymized data:

  • Number of app sessions
  • Duration of app use
  • Feature interactions
  • Device type and OS version

Firebase does NOT collect: Your SMS content, contacts, phone number, or personal data.

Firebase may store data in Google's servers (including outside India). For details, see: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Opt Out: Settings > About > Privacy Settings > Toggle off "Analytics Collection"

6.2 Crash Reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)

When the app crashes, Firebase Crashlytics collects:

  • Technical error information (stack traces)

  • App version

Crash reports do NOT include: Your SMS content or personal data.

6.3 No Advertising

Tidy SMS is completely ad-free. I do not use ads, do not collect data for ad networks, and do not sell your data to advertisers.

7. DATA SECURITY

I implement reasonable security measures:

  • Device Encryption: Your SMS and contacts are protected by Android's built-in security
  • Secure Connection: Data sent to my servers uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Limited Access: Only I can access analytics data
  • Regular Updates: I keep the app and dependencies updated for security

However, no system is 100% secure. If you suspect your data has been compromised, email me immediately.

8. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER INDIAN LAW

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023, you have these rights:

8.1 Right to Access

You can ask for all personal data I hold about you. I will respond within 30 days.

8.2 Right to Correction

If any data is wrong or incomplete, you can ask me to fix it.

8.3 Right to Erasure

  • If you want to delete Tidy SMS data from your device, you can use Android’s built in option: go to Settings → Apps → Tidy SMS → Storage and tap Clear storage / Clear data. This removes Tidy SMS local databases, caches and settings from your phone.

  • Tidy SMS does not create or control your Google account backups. SMS and app backups stored in Google Drive are managed by Google’s Android backup system and your device settings. You can delete those backups at any time from Google Drive or your phone’s Backup settings.

  • Some minimal diagnostic data may be retained for up to 90 days where required for security, fraud prevention or legal reasons, after which it is deleted or irreversibly anonymised.

Note: Some data may be retained for legal reasons (e.g., crash reports up to 90 days per my policy).

8.4 Right to Grievance Redressal

If you're unhappy with how I handle your data:

  1. Email me with details of your complaint
  2. I will acknowledge it within 15-30 days
  3. I will investigate and respond within 30 days

To file a complaint, email: gakshayapps@gmail.com Include: Your name, complaint description, and what resolution you seek.

8.5 Right to Data Portability

You can request all your analytics data in a downloadable format. I will provide it within 30 days.
